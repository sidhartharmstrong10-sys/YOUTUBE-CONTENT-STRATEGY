# Why I Hire People With No Tech Background

Channel: Talking Too Loud (Wistia)
Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aqLv7k8hsY8
Upload date: 2026-06-09

---

I'm in tech, and I'm hiring people who weren't supposed to be here. Like, not even close. Before tech, they were working as things like a spotlight operator for Lady Gaga, a Broadway performer, a reality TV contestant, a goat tender, a Christmas tree decorator, a Rome Hits bookstore owner, a therapist, a bowling alley mechanic. None of that sounds like tech, and that is the point. The people winning right now aren't the ones with the perfect background. They're the ones who know how to figure things out when there's no playbook. They're creative. They're scrappy. They adapt fast. They're the ones who figured out how to move faster than everyone else. What did you do before this? Comment before and after.
