## Cross-list observations (relevant to the brief)

- **Practitioner-first, not exec-first:** 30MPC (#5) and Ross Simmonds (#9) skew most directly toward tactical, practitioner-facing content — closest fit to the stated audience.
- **Solo-founder-realistic production:** Dave Gerhardt/Exit Five (#1), Ross Simmonds (#9), and Rob Walling (#10) look the most replicable without a production team (talking-head / screen-share style) vs. Marketing Against the Grain or SaaStr, which have network/event backing.
- **"Company show as sales enablement" model:** Ian Faison (Caspian) and Chris Savage (Wistia) are useful less as content to copy and more as proof that a well-produced show can double as a demo of the company's own product/service.
